# Stakeholder Interviews

This folder contains documentation of **stakeholder communications and interviews** for your OCR NEA project.

## What to Include

### Interview Records
- Interview transcripts or notes
- Audio recordings (if permitted)
- Video recordings (if permitted)
- Interview questions and responses
- Date and duration of each interview

### Communication Records
- Email exchanges
- Meeting notes
- Feedback forms
- Phone call notes
- Chat/messaging conversations

### Stakeholder Feedback
- Requirements gathering
- Design feedback
- Feature requests
- Testing feedback
- Final evaluation

### Consent and Documentation
- Permission to use information
- Stakeholder details (anonymized if needed)
- Contact information
- Role/position

## Interview Documentation Format

```markdown
# Stakeholder Interview - [Date]

**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Time:** HH:MM
**Duration:** X minutes
**Stakeholder:** [Name or "Anonymous"]
**Role:** [Their role/position]
**Interview Method:** Face-to-face / Phone / Video call / Email

## Purpose
Brief description of what this interview aimed to achieve.

## Questions and Responses

### Q1: [Question]
**Response:** [Stakeholder's answer]
**Notes:** [Your observations or clarifications]

### Q2: [Question]
**Response:** [Stakeholder's answer]
**Notes:** [Your observations or clarifications]

## Key Takeaways
- Important point 1
- Important point 2
- Important point 3

## Requirements Identified
1. [Requirement 1]
2. [Requirement 2]

## Next Steps
- Follow-up actions
- Clarifications needed
- Next meeting/communication planned

## Additional Notes
Any other relevant information
```

## Types of Stakeholder Interactions

### 1. Initial Requirements Gathering
- Understanding the problem
- Identifying needs
- Defining scope
- Success criteria

### 2. Design Feedback
- Review of wireframes
- Review of architecture plans
- UI/UX feedback
- Technical approach validation

### 3. Development Check-ins
- Progress demonstrations
- Feature feedback
- Change requests
- Priority adjustments

### 4. Testing Sessions
- User acceptance testing
- Usability feedback
- Bug reports
- Feature validation

### 5. Final Evaluation
- Overall satisfaction
- Success criteria review
- Suggestions for improvement
- Final approval

## Interview Best Practices

### Preparation
- Prepare questions in advance
- Schedule appropriate time
- Choose quiet location
- Test recording equipment (if using)

### During Interview
- Listen actively
- Take detailed notes
- Ask follow-up questions
- Confirm understanding
- Be professional

### After Interview
- Write up notes immediately
- Clarify any unclear points
- Thank stakeholder
- Plan next steps

## Sample Interview Questions

### Initial Requirements
- What problem are you trying to solve?
- Who will use this system?
- What features are essential?
- What are your success criteria?
- Are there any constraints?

### Design Feedback
- Does this design meet your needs?
- Is the interface intuitive?
- Are there any missing features?
- Is anything confusing or unclear?

### Testing Feedback
- Does the system work as expected?
- Are you satisfied with the results?
- What would you like changed?
- Any bugs or issues found?

## Privacy and Ethics

### Data Protection
- Get permission to record/document
- Anonymize personal data if required
- Follow GDPR guidelines
- Protect stakeholder privacy

### Professional Conduct
- Be respectful and professional
- Be honest about capabilities
- Don't make unrealistic promises
- Value their time

## Assessment Focus

Examiners look for:
- Evidence of genuine stakeholder engagement
- Regular communication throughout project
- Requirements based on stakeholder needs
- Feedback incorporated into development
- Professional documentation

## What NOT to Do

- ❌ Fabricate stakeholder interactions
- ❌ Only include positive feedback
- ❌ Wait until end to gather all evidence
- ❌ Include personal/sensitive information
- ❌ Make vague or generic notes

## Checklist

- [ ] Initial interview conducted
- [ ] Requirements documented
- [ ] Design feedback obtained
- [ ] Regular progress updates
- [ ] Testing feedback collected
- [ ] Final evaluation completed
- [ ] All communications dated
- [ ] Permission obtained for recordings

## Tips

- **Document everything**: Keep all communications
- **Be timely**: Write notes while fresh
- **Be thorough**: Capture details
- **Be professional**: All interactions should be appropriate
- **Show evolution**: Demonstrate ongoing relationship
- **Get feedback regularly**: Don't wait for the end
